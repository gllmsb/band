About The Vaccines

The Vaccines are a British indie rock band formed in West London in 2010. Known for their energetic and catchy guitar-driven sound, they quickly gained attention with their debut album "What Did You Expect from The Vaccines?" released in 2011. The band consists of Justin Hayward-Young (vocals, guitar), Freddie Cowan (guitar, vocals), Árni Árnason (bass, vocals), and Yoann Intonti (drums, percussion). With a style influenced by post-punk revival and garage rock, The Vaccines have been recognized for their infectious hooks, dynamic performances, and witty lyricism.
